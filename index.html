SCRABBLE

Drag tiles from tile rack onto the board to create a word. Press Submit to submit word or Reset to start over.

Rules:

1. Submit is only available if there are at least TWO tiles on the board and when word is valid

2. Place tiles next to one another, words with spaces in between are not valid

3. When the word is valid, the submit button will light up. Press to commit your word and to earn your points

4. There are a limited number of tiles available, there is a different distribution for each tile

5. Using board squares with "Double Word Score" will double your score for the current round
